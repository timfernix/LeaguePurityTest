timfernix' League Purity Test v2.0

Last updated: August 23th 2024 @ 11:31AM EET

Welcome to my League of Legends Purity Test. This test is designed to gauge your LoL experience and habits. Simply click on all the statements that apply to you. As you go through the test, you'll notice a mix of positive and negative statements. That's because your score can be negative if you've made some... questionable choices in-game. Once you've clicked on all the statements that apply to you, choose a background image to personalize your results (at least a little bit). Finally, click on "Show Results" to see your score!

Note: Some of the questions are inspired by my first version of the League Purity Test that i posted on Twitter ,but i added many more to make it even better. So, be honest and have fun!

Sportmanship

  • Have you ever encouraged your teammates with positive comments during a game?
  • Have you ever complimented an opponent on their good play or a well-executed strategy?
  • Have you ever chosen not to kill a disconnected or AFK player, even when you had the opportunity?
  • Have you ever started a lighthearted or humorous conversation with an enemy player during a game?
  • Have you ever complimented a player for their skin or chroma?
  • Have you ever gone out of your lane to protect your Support's ward from being destroyed?
  • Have you ever jumped in front of an ability (such as Caitlyn's ultimate) to save a teammate from taking dying?
