Welcome to my League of Legends Purity Test. This test is designed to gauge your League of Legends experience and habits. Simply click on all the statements that apply to you. As you go through the test, you'll notice a mix of positive and negative statements. That's because your score can be negative if you've made some... questionable choices in-game. Once you've clicked on all the statements that apply to you, enter your name, choose a background image to personalize your results (at least a little bit). Finally, click on "Show Results" to see your score!

Some of the questions are inspired by my first version of the League Purity Test that i posted on / Twitter, but i added many more to make it even better. So, be honest and have fun!

Note: Be aware that this is a subjective test, as it was only created by me and therefore based on my experience and just to have a bit of fun with it. I look forward to any feedback under my announcement post on / Twitter Thank you very much!
